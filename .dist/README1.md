
# YIELDSENCE AI

## The problem :

"Farmers currently rely a lot on intuition and historical patterns to make planting decisions — which crop, how much fertilizer, when to plant. That breaks down under real climate volatility, and it leads to over-fertilization, poor crop choices, and water waste. 

## Aim:
The aim of YieldSense is to replace that guesswork with actual data — soil readings, coordinates, weather — feeding a machine learning model that forecasts yield in kilograms per hectare."
